# Catastrophe Generated Asset Atlas

The PNG files in this directory are generated by `scripts/generate_assets_atlas.py`.
They preserve the sprite coordinates used by the former NextUI-compatible status/control
atlas, but the artwork is redrawn for Catastrophe.

Semantic glyphs are derived from Tabler Icons v3.44.0:

- Source: https://github.com/tabler/tabler-icons
- Icon browser: https://tabler.io/icons
- License: MIT

The generator references these Tabler icon concepts by name: `brightness`, `sun`,
`temperature`, `volume`, `volume-2`, `battery`, `bolt`, `chevron-up`,
`chevron-down`, `wifi`, `wifi-off`, `bluetooth`, `bluetooth-off`, `headphones`,
`device-gamepad-2`, `circle-check`, `lock`, `settings`, `building-store`,
`power`, `refresh`, and `moon`.
